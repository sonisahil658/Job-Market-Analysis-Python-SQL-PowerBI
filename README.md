# Job Market Analysis – Data Science Hiring Trends in the United States

## Project Overview

This project analyzes 742 Data Science job postings from across the United States to understand hiring trends, salary patterns, skill demand, industry hiring behavior, and geographic job distribution. The analysis was performed using Python, MySQL, and Power BI to generate business insights and support data-driven decision making for both employers and job seekers.

---

## Business Problem Statement

The Data Science job market is growing rapidly across industries, but hiring trends, salary expectations, required skills, and location-based opportunities vary significantly. Organizations need a better understanding of workforce demand to improve recruitment strategies, while professionals need insights into the most valuable skills, industries, and locations for career growth.

This project aims to analyze job market data and identify patterns in hiring, compensation, technical skill requirements, education preferences, and industry demand.

---

## Project Deliverables

### 1. Data Preparation & Modeling (Python)

Clean and transform raw job market data, handle missing values, perform feature engineering, and prepare the dataset for analysis.

### 2. Data Analysis (SQL)

Perform business-focused analysis to identify hiring trends, salary distributions, skill demand, company hiring activity, and geographic job concentration.

### 3. Visualization & Insights (Power BI)

Build an interactive dashboard to visualize job market trends, salary analysis, industry demand, and technical skill requirements.

### 4. Report & Presentation

Document key findings, insights, and recommendations derived from the analysis.

### 5. GitHub Repository

Maintain all project files including Python scripts, SQL queries, Power BI dashboards, reports, and project documentation.

## Key Insights

* California has the highest number of Data Science job openings.
* Python is the most demanded technical skill across job postings.
* Biotech & Pharmaceuticals is one of the leading hiring industries.
* Machine Learning and Director-level roles offer the highest salaries.
* More than half of the jobs focus on skills rather than degree requirements.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Job seekers should prioritize Python, SQL, and cloud technologies.
* Employers should adopt skill-based hiring strategies.
* Organizations can expand hiring beyond traditional tech hubs to access talent at lower costs.
* Companies should benchmark salaries based on market demand to remain competitive.
